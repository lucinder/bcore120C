    On the Inevitable Tragedy of Heroism

    A poem made of three 14-word stanzas, and a brief analysis thereof.

    15 May, 2021


    Mighty Theseus’ tale is well-known, And though many a grand skill he honed The Athenian king Forgot one crucial thing: All great heroes die scorned and alone. Though his honor was plenty His followers many He can never return back home.
